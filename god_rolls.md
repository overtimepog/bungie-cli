## Perk priority this season
- **PvE swap-DPS is king:** Envious Arsenal + Bait and Switch (enhanced = new bar). Ambitious Assassin + Chain Reaction for add-clear. Cascade Point, Precision Instrument (+25%), Frenzy, Explosive Light, Vorpal, Reservoir Burst, Auto-Loading.
- **Stasis synergy:** Headstone/Crystalline + Rhyme Stealer.
- **Archetype buffs:** aggressive shotguns & high-impact fusions +15% PvE.
- **PvP:** consistency/TTK — Rapid Hit, Moving Target, Kill Clip, Opening Shot, Rangefinder, Perpetual Motion, Headseeker; Range/Stability MW; chase Adepts.
- Separate **structural** rolls (always good) from **temporal** ones spiked by the current artifact (Chain Reaction, Voltshot, Destabilizing Rounds).
